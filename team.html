Our Team

We are a group of individuals united by a shared passion to provide high-quality digital solutions to these underserved health workers and the communities they serve. More than 75% of our core team lives or has lived in Sub-Saharan Africa and have been directly impacted with devastating effect by the inefficiencies of the health systems currently in place. This has served as a motivation for us to pursue our mission diligence and focus.

Our Team

Technology

As a Dublin City University alumni where he was awarded a BSc degree in Enterprise Computing (Computing and Business), Rory Assandey has accumulated over 9 years of experience leading and technically contributing to large and highly visible enterprise IT projects. Rory’s rooted emotional connection to healthcare and social change comes from having had a front-row seat as his mother, Shirley Assandey, and aunt, Lorna Kingston, lead a maternity hospital in a remote town in the South-East region of Cote d’Ivoire for almost 30 years combined.
